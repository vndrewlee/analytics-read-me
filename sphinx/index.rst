Analytics!
============================================

This is a living document. If you'd like to contribute, something Github.

Analytics and data work is a growing field, but without a well worn path forward. A Lunascape logo cloud isn't very helpful. Successful analytics requires drawing upon a wide range a fields; such as design, math, statistics, and engineering. Tacit knowledge and knowhow is also important for whatever field you are applying your trade.

There are both abstract concepts and approaches, as well as practical skills to learn.
